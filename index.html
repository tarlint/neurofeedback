What is Neurofeedback?

Neurofeedback, also known as EEG biofeedback, is a non-invasive therapy that uses real-time displays of brain activity to teach self-regulation of brain function. Through immediate feedback about brainwave patterns, individuals can learn to modify their brain activity and improve cognitive performance.

This evidence-based therapy has been used successfully for decades to address a wide range of conditions including ADHD, anxiety, depression, sleep disorders, and cognitive enhancement.

Benefits of Neurofeedback

  • Improved focus and attention
  • Better emotional regulation
  • Enhanced sleep quality
  • Reduced anxiety and stress
  • Increased cognitive performance
  • Non-invasive and drug-free
